About PPMG 'Akad. Ivan Tsenov'

PPMG 'Akad. Ivan Tsenov' is a prestigious mathematics and science high school in Vratsa, Bulgaria, with a tradition of academic excellence dating back to 1971. We are committed to providing high-quality education in mathematics, informatics, physics, and natural sciences.

Our school takes pride in participating in the Erasmus+ program, fostering international collaboration and providing our students with opportunities to engage in educational projects across Europe. Through these initiatives, our students develop not only academic skills but also cultural awareness, language proficiency, and global citizenship.

At PPMG 'Akad. Ivan Tsenov', we believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who excel academically while also developing critical soft skills through international cooperation and exchange programs.

Erasmus+ Projects (2024–2027)

Explore our exciting projects and collaborations with schools across Europe.

STEM Digital Ongoing

Digital Innovation in STEM Education

A collaborative project focusing on innovative digital tools and methodologies for STEM education. Students from five European countries work together to develop digital learning resources and conduct joint research projects.

Partner Countries: Bulgaria, Germany, Spain, Finland, Estonia

Duration: September 2024 - August 2026

Environment Sustainability Ongoing

Sustainable Future: Youth in Action

This project aims to raise awareness about environmental issues and sustainability among young people. Students collaborate on developing local and international initiatives addressing climate change, biodiversity, and sustainable living.

Partner Countries: Bulgaria, Sweden, Portugal, France, Greece

Duration: January 2024 - December 2025

Culture Digital Upcoming

Cultural Heritage in the Digital Age

An innovative project focusing on preserving and promoting local cultural heritage through digital tools. Students will create virtual exhibitions, documentary films, and digital archives of their local traditions and landmarks.

Partner Countries: Bulgaria, Italy, Czech Republic, Poland, Romania

Duration: September 2025 - August 2027

Entrepreneurship Innovation Upcoming

Entrepreneurship and Innovation for Youth

This project aims to develop entrepreneurial skills and innovative thinking among high school students. Participants will work on creating business plans, prototypes, and startup ideas with guidance from industry experts and mentors.

Partner Countries: Bulgaria, Netherlands, Denmark, Estonia, Ireland

Duration: January 2025 - December 2026

Digital Innovation in STEM Education
Project Description

This collaborative project focuses on innovative digital tools and methodologies for STEM education. Students from five European countries work together to develop digital learning resources and conduct joint research projects.

Objectives
  • Develop innovative digital tools for STEM education
  • Enhance students' digital competencies
  • Create collaborative learning environments
  • Share best practices between partner schools
  • Improve teaching methodologies in STEM subjects
Activities
  • International student exchanges
  • Virtual collaboration platforms
  • Development of digital learning resources
  • Research projects on innovative STEM topics
  • Teacher training workshops
  • Final conference and presentation of results
Partner Schools
  • PPMG 'Akad. Ivan Tsenov', Vratsa, Bulgaria (Coordinator)
  • Gymnasium Berlin-Steglitz, Berlin, Germany
  • IES Santiago Ramón y Cajal, Madrid, Spain
  • Helsingin matematiikkalukio, Helsinki, Finland
  • Tallinna Reaalkool, Tallinn, Estonia
