About our school

The Secondary School of Science and Mathematics "Akademician Ivan Tsenov" in Vratsa is one of the leading educational institutions in Bulgaria, renowned for its strong emphasis on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). Founded with a vision of fostering academic excellence, the school has consistently upheld its reputation as a center for intellectual growth and innovation. Named after the esteemed Bulgarian academician Ivan Tsenov, the school embodies a commitment to providing students with a solid foundation in mathematics and the natural sciences, alongside a well-rounded education in the humanities and social sciences. Its modern facilities include specialized laboratories, computer rooms, and a library, offering students access to advanced resources and technology. The school promotes a culture of academic achievement and innovation by actively participating in national and international competitions in mathematics, physics, computer science, and other disciplines. Many of its students have earned prestigious awards, demonstrating their exceptional skills and knowledge. Beyond academics, the school encourages holistic development through extracurricular activities such as sports, arts, cultural programs and European programs, such as Erasmus+, EPAS (European Parliament Ambassador Schools), OSOS (Open Schools for Open Societies) and ENEN ( European Nuclear Education Network) and Scientix. It serves as a hub for nurturing future leaders, scientists, and professionals, instilling in its students values of discipline, curiosity, and lifelong learning. With its dedicated faculty and commitment to excellence, the Secondary School of Science and Mathematics "Akademician Ivan Tsenov" continues to be a source of pride for Vratsa and a beacon of educational distinction in Bulgaria.
