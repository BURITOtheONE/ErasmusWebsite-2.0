About our Program

STR5EAM Erasmus+ @ PPMG - Vratsa

The Secondary School of Science and Mathematics "Akademician Ivan Tsenov" - Vratsa is a holder of long-term accreditation under the Erasmus+ program (KA120-SCH). The main goal of our project is to transform the learning environment through an innovative and interdisciplinary approach to STEM education, complemented by arts and responsible research.

What is STR5EAM?

STR5EAM is an upgrade of the traditional STEM model that includes five key R-components and the arts (Art), placing students at the center of learning through creation, discovery, and teamwork.

  • Recreation - learning through games, escape rooms, puzzles and fun science activities
  • Reading - developing functional literacy and understanding of scientific texts
  • Robotics - learning digital and algorithmic skills through robotics
  • Relation - solving real social and environmental problems through STEM projects
  • Research - building skills for responsible research and innovation
  • Art - using visual and performing arts to express scientific ideas

Our vision

STR5EAM is an educational model that prepares students not just to acquire knowledge, but to create, communicate, and implement solutions in the real world. The project stimulates creativity, teamwork, and social responsibility.

Thanks to Erasmus+ accreditation, students and teachers participate in international training, exchanges and activities that build new horizons and develop key skills of the future.

The core of the Erasmus+ project lies in building strong international relationships between educational facilities. Through various exchange programs, schools and universities work together on joint projects, share teaching methods, and explore innovative approaches to learning.

These partnerships encourage the exchange of knowledge, culture, and experiences, allowing participants to develop a deeper understanding of different educational systems and perspectives. Students and educators benefit from working in diverse environments, enhancing their communication skills, teamwork, and global awareness.

In addition to fostering collaboration, Erasmus+ promotes the development of key competencies and skills that are essential for success in today's interconnected world. Participants engage in various activities, including workshops, seminars, and cultural exchanges, which help them acquire valuable skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, and adaptability.

Erasmus+ promotes unity through education, helping to create a network of schools that are open, cooperative, and committed to improving learning across borders.

About our school

The Secondary School of Science and Mathematics "Akademician Ivan Tsenov" in Vratsa is one of the leading educational institutions in Bulgaria, renowned for its strong emphasis on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). Founded with a vision of fostering academic excellence, the school has consistently upheld its reputation as a center for intellectual growth and innovation.

Named after the esteemed Bulgarian academician Ivan Tsenov, the school embodies a commitment to providing students with a solid foundation in mathematics and the natural sciences, alongside a well-rounded education in the humanities and social sciences. Its modern facilities include specialized laboratories, computer rooms, and a library, offering students access to advanced resources and technology.

The school promotes a culture of academic achievement and innovation by actively participating in national and international competitions in mathematics, physics, computer science, and other disciplines. Many of its students have earned prestigious awards, demonstrating their exceptional skills and knowledge.

Beyond academics, the school encourages holistic development through extracurricular activities such as sports, arts, cultural programs and European programs, such as Erasmus+, EPAS (European Parliament Ambassador Schools), OSOS (Open Schools for Open Societies) and ENEN ( European Nuclear Education Network) and Scientix. It serves as a hub for nurturing future leaders, scientists, and professionals, instilling in its students values of discipline, curiosity, and lifelong learning.

With its dedicated faculty and commitment to excellence, the Secondary School of Science and Mathematics "Akademician Ivan Tsenov" continues to be a source of pride for Vratsa and a beacon of educational distinction in Bulgaria.
