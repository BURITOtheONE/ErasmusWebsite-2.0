About the School

The Secondary School of Science and Mathematics "Akademician Ivan Tsenov" in Vratsa is one of the leading educational institutions in Bulgaria, renowned for its strong emphasis on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). Founded with a vision of fostering academic excellence, the school has consistently upheld its reputation as a center for intellectual growth and innovation.
